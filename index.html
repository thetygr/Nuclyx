Tritovex
Spray Encapsulation Technology
A sprayable aqueous suspension delivered by radiation-hardened robot directly onto contaminated corium surfaces. Upon application, Tritovex self-assembles into a durable encapsulation barrier with ultra-low radionuclide permeability - five orders of magnitude below conventional materials. The formulation simultaneously achieves chemical immobilization and physical sealing, and the barrier autonomously restores integrity following radiation-induced damage.
